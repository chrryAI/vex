## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### About Revenue

**Q: Is there a cap on how much I can earn?**
A: No. You earn 70% of everything your apps generate, with no upper limit.

**Q: What if I build multiple apps?**
A: You earn 70% from each app separately. Build 5 apps generating $2k/month each = $7k/month total earnings.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Monthly via Stripe Connect, with a $50 minimum. First payment within 30 days of reaching minimum.

**Q: What if someone extends my app?**
A: You still earn 70% when users use your app, even if accessed through a child app.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. 70/30 split is all-inclusive. Platform covers Stripe fees, hosting, everything.

### About Development

**Q: Do I need my own AI API keys?**
A: No. Platform provides API access by default. You can use your own keys to maximize your 70% if you prefer.

**Q: Can I use external services?**
A: Yes. You can integrate any third-party API, but you're responsible for those costs.

**Q: What tech stack must I use?**
A: Next.js, React, TypeScript are core. Beyond that, you have flexibility within the monorepo structure.

**Q: Can I work on other projects?**
A: Yes. No exclusivity. Build for other platforms, have a day job, whatever works for you.

**Q: How much time commitment?**
A: Flexible. Could be 5 hours/week or full-time. You set your own schedule.

### About Support

**Q: What if I get stuck technically?**
A: Email iliyan@chrry.ai or review docs. 24-hour response time guaranteed.

**Q: Do I handle customer support for my apps?**
A: No. Platform handles general support. You only handle app-specific technical issues.

**Q: What if my app has a bug?**
A: You're responsible for fixing bugs in your code. Platform handles infrastructure issues.

### About Rights

**Q: Who owns the code I write?**
A: You do. Full ownership of your apps and intellectual property.

**Q: Can I take my app to another platform?**
A: Yes, after termination. During active participation, apps remain on Chrry.

**Q: What if Chrry shuts down?**
A: Builder Agreement includes data export rights and 12-month revenue continuation.

### About Scaling

**Q: What happens if my app goes viral?**
A: Platform scales automatically. Your 70% remains unchanged regardless of volume.

**Q: Can I hire other developers?**
A: Yes. You can form a team and split your 70% however you want.

**Q: Can I raise funding for my app?**
A: Yes. Your app is your business. You control fundraising, pricing, roadmap.
